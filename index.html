Swimming Poem

In the crystal clear waters, I dive and play,
With each stroke of my fins, I dance away.
The ripples on the surface, they shimmer and glow,
As I swim deeper, the world below.
The ocean's depths, so vast and blue,
A treasure trove of secrets, waiting for you.
Schools of fish, they dart and weave,
Their scales shining bright, like diamonds in the wave.
The coral reefs, a colorful sight,
Teeming with life, both day and night.
Sea turtles glide, with graceful ease,
Through the currents, they find their peace.
